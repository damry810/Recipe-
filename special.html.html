How to Prepare Zobo Drink

Ingredients:

  • Zobo Leaves: 2 cups (dried hibiscus petals)  
  • Water: 4 cups
  • Ginger: 1-inch piece, peeled and sliced
  • Cloves: 1 teaspoon
  • Pineapple: 1/4 cup, peeled and chopped (optional)
  • Sugar or Honey: To taste
  • Natural Flavors: (optional) - such as pineapple, orange, or lemon  

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the Zobo Leaves: Thoroughly wash the zobo leaves under running water to remove any dust or impurities.
  2. Boil the Zobo Leaves: In a pot, combine the zobo leaves, ginger, cloves, and pineapple (if using) with 4 cups of water. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Simmer: Reduce heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until the zobo leaves are soft and the liquid has turned a deep red color.
  4. Cool and Strain: Remove the pot from heat and let the mixture cool to room temperature. Strain the liquid through a fine-mesh sieve or cheesecloth to remove the zobo leaves, ginger, and cloves.
  5. Sweeten and Flavor: Add sugar or honey to taste, and stir well to dissolve. If desired, add natural flavors like pineapple, orange, or lemon juice.
  6. Chill and Serve: Chill the zobo drink in the refrigerator for a few hours or serve it over ice cubes.

Tips:

  • For a stronger flavor, use more zobo leaves.
  • You can adjust the amount of water to achieve your desired concentration.
  • To make a larger batch, simply increase the proportions of the ingredients.
  • Zobo can also be served warm or hot.
Enjoy your refreshing and healthy zobo drink!
