How to prepare Rice and Beans Porridge

Here's a recipe for Rice and Beans Porridge:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried brown or black-eyed peas, picked and washed
  • 1 cup long-grain rice, washed
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper (or to taste), blended
  • 1 tablespoon palm oil or vegetable oil
  • 2-3 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Instructions:

    Parboil the Beans:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Add the washed beans and cook for about 15-20 minutes, or until slightly softened.
  3. Drain the water and set the beans aside.

Sauté the Aromatics:

  1. Heat the oil in a pot.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic and sauté until softened and fragrant.
  3. Add the blended pepper and cook for a few minutes more.

Combine and Cook:

  1. Add the parboiled beans, washed rice, seasoning cubes, and salt to the pot.
  2. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients by about 2 inches.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 45-60 minutes, or until the rice and beans are tender and the liquid has thickened to your desired consistency.

Tips:

  • For a richer flavor, you can add smoked fish or meat to the porridge.
  • You can adjust the amount of water to achieve your desired consistency.
  • If the porridge is too thick, add a little more water and simmer until it reaches the desired consistency.
  • You can also add other vegetables like carrots, green beans, or spinach to the porridge.
Enjoy your delicious and nutritious Rice and Beans Porridge!
