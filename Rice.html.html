JOLLOF RICE

A very quick and most easy way to make jollof rice, tasty and so delicious. Do well to leave comments after trying it out.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked rice
  • Tin Tomatoes
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Fresh Pepper
  • Native Pepper (Shombo)
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Onions
  • Seasoning(Salt, Maggi, Curry, Bay leaf)
  • Stock(meat stock or fish stock)

Preparations

  1. Start by parboiling the raw rice and set it aside.
  2. Set the pot on the fire for it to get dry and put the Groundnut oil. (Preferable to use the oil used in frying the protein).
  3. When the oil us hot, put the onions and fry a little while.
  4. Then add your tin tomatoes and fry for like 10 minutes until you notice it's getting dark and add your bay leaf.
  5. Then you add the fresh tomatoes and fry for 5 minutes.
  6. Add your seasoning and stock.
  7. Allow it boil for some minutes before adding your parboiled rice and add water enough to make it soft(Not too soft okay?).
  8. Keep checking it and adjust the seasoning to taste.
  9. Some people love adding onions and butter when the water is dried, so you can add one too.
