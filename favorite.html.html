How to Make Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • Meat: 1 pound chicken breast, thinly sliced, or 1 pound beef, thinly sliced

    • Marinade:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

    • Vegetables:

  • 1/2 head of cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • Wraps: 4-6 pita bread or tortillas

    • Sauces:

  • Hummus
  • Tahini sauce
  • Hot sauce (optional)

Instructions:

Marinate the Meat:

  1. In a bowl, combine the marinade ingredients. Add the chicken or beef and marinate for at least 30 minutes, or up to 24 hours in the refrigerator.
  2. Grill or Pan-fry the Meat: Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Grill or pan-fry the marinated meat until cooked through and slightly charred.
  3. Assemble the Shawarma: Warm the pita bread or tortillas on a griddle or in a skillet. Spread hummus or tahini sauce on the inside of each wrap. Layer the grilled meat, vegetables, and any additional toppings you like (such as hot sauce or pickled turnips).

Tips:

  • For extra flavor, you can marinate the meat overnight.
  • If you don't have a grill, you can use a large skillet to cook the meat.
  • You can use any type of vegetables you like in your shawarma.
  • Get creative with your sauces! Try different combinations of hummus, tahini, hot sauce, and other condiments.
Enjoy your delicious homemade shawarma!
